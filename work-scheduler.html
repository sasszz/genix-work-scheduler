How to use:
1. Add your recurring onsite locations above — they'll be remembered next time.

2. Each Friday, pick the week date, click days to assign locations, then hit Add to Google Calendar.

3. Each event opens in Google Calendar pre-filled — just confirm to save.

Tip: Click a day repeatedly to cycle through locations. Click the assigned location again to clear it.
